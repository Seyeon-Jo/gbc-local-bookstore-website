About Our Store

Daily Books

"Daily Books" is local and independent bookstore. We also have online bookstore to provide better services for customers. We believe that book is essential to have healthy daily life. This connects readers with authors and we discover new writers. Ad more and more people buy their books online, we wanted to create an easy, convenient way for you to get your books and support bookstores at the same time.

It's time to read!

"A day without a book is like a latte without a coffee."

Coffee is an essential ingredient of a latte. Like this, a Book is an important part in our daily lives. Let's read a book and make healthy daily life.
